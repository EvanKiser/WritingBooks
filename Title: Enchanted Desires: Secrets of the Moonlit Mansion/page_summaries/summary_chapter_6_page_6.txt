In the Moonlit Mansion, Valarie seeks advice from Hugo, the enigmatic butler, about an enchantment affecting her and her friends. Hugo tells her that the power to overcome the enchantment lies within her, and she should trust her instincts. Meanwhile, Elena and Liam worry if their connection will remain once the enchantment fades. As the four friends search for a solution, a mysterious figure watches them from the shadows. United by their courage and determination, they continue to face the challenges together, knowing that time is running out.
