Elena and Liam, two characters who fell in love under an enchantment, discuss their feelings for each other and the future of their relationship now that the enchantment has been lifted. Meanwhile, their friends Valarie and Jasper ponder whether the enchantment changed them or simply revealed their hidden desires. As Elena and Liam vow to face the challenges of the future together, Valarie hopes that the clarity they gained from the enchantment will not fade away once they leave the Moonlit Mansion.
