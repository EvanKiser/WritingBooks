The four friends, Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper, stand in the moonlit chamber of the Moonlit Mansion, reflecting on their transformative journey and the deep connections they've formed. Having faced their fears and broken the enchantments, they notice subtle changes in their surroundings, signifying the lifting of a great burden. As they prepare to leave, they acknowledge their newfound strength and confidence, ready to face any challenges life may bring. The enchantments may be gone, but the indelible marks they left on the friends have forever changed their lives, intertwining their fates and strengthening their emotional bonds.
