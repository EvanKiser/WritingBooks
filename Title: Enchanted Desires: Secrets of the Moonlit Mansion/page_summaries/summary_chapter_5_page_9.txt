In the hidden library of the Moonlit Mansion, four companions, Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper, discover an ancient enchantment that manipulates emotions and binds them to the mansion's garden. Realizing the sinister origins of the enchantment, they become determined to break free from its control and regain control of their destinies. As they search for a solution, they find strength in their friendships and connections, vowing to face the challenges together.
