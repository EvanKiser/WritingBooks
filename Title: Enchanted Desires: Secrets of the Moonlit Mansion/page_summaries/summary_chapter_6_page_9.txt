In the Moonlit Mansion, four friends bound by enchantments and their connections to one another, embark on a journey to uncover the secrets behind these enchantments. As they study ancient texts and explore hidden passages, they grow closer and realize the enchantments are part of a larger tapestry woven by a mysterious figure. Despite their fears, the friends rely on their love and friendship to face the truth and confront the price of pleasure, reclaiming control over their lives and destinies.
