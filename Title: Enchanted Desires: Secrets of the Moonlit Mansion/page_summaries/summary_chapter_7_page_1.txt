Valarie, struggling with her feelings for Jasper and her own sexuality, seeks solace and advice from her friend Elena. As the mansion buzzes with activity, Valarie discovers a diary of the previous owner, Eliza, detailing her tumultuous love life and the consequences of enchantments. Meanwhile, Elena and Liam face their challenges together, bound by love and trust. Valarie, now armed with a better understanding of the enchantments, decides it's time to confront the truth with the others.
