Valarie and Jasper share their fears and determination to face enchantments and self-discovery on the library's balcony. Inside, Elena and Liam study ancient grimoires, seeking ways to protect their love and friendships. As morning comes, the group unites in their mission, and a sorceress named Aurelia joins them as their guide. Together, they prepare to confront the past and the enchantments that have shaped their lives, hoping to unlock the secrets of their hearts and souls.
