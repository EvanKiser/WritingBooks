Under the moonlight, four friends prepare to leave the enchanting Moonlit Mansion, reflecting on the personal growth and deep connections they experienced during their stay. Jasper and Valarie express gratitude for their newfound courage and the power of friendship, while Elena and Liam find strength in their love and the belief that they can face any challenges together. As they leave the mansion, they carry with them the memories, lessons, and unbreakable bonds formed during their magical journey.
