Elena and Liam discover a hidden library filled with ancient books, which ignites a sense of magic and wonder within them, as well as a shared sense of purpose. They feel an unbreakable bond forming between them as they explore the library's vast collection. Valarie, watching from the shadows, feels a mixture of longing and envy for their connection, but Jasper reassures her that there's a world of possibility waiting for her too. Together, they all delve deeper into the library, uncovering enchantments and feeling a sense of magic bloom within them.
