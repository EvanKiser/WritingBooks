Under the moonlight, four friends embark on an emotional journey to uncover the secrets of enchantments from an ancient tome. As they delve deeper, each friend faces their personal histories and suppressed emotions, while a mysterious figure lurks in the shadows. Their love and determination fuel their quest, and they realize that breaking the enchantment is crucial for their self-discovery and relationships. Together, they resolve to confront the enchantments head-on, as the Moonlit Mansion awaits the next chapter in their lives.
