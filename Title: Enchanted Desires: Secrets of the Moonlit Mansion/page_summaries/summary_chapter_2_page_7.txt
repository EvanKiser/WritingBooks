Elena and Liam discover ancient tomes with mysterious symbols that hint at powerful enchantments hidden within the walls of the Moonlit Mansion. As they explore further, they come across a large manuscript that seems to hold the key to unlocking these secrets. Meanwhile, Valarie and Jasper, who are observing them from the shadows, feel a deep yearning to experience the same magic. Together, the four friends prepare to embark on a journey to uncover the hidden power within the mansion, ready to embrace the enchantments and change their lives forever.
