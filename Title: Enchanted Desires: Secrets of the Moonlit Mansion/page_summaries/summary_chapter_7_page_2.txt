In a dimly lit library, Valarie discovers an ancient journal belonging to a woman named Eliza, who once lived in the Moonlit Mansion and faced challenges due to enchantments. As Valarie reads Eliza's story, she wonders if she and her friends will face a similar fate. Meanwhile, Elena and Liam discuss their love and the role of enchantments in their relationship, while Jasper seeks Elena's advice on supporting Valarie's growth. As the friends uncover the truth about their connections through Eliza's diary, they prepare to face the consequences of their actions and enchantments, united in love and trust.
