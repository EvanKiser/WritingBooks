Under the moonlit sky, Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper find themselves at the edge of the enchanting garden surrounding the Moonlit Mansion, reflecting on their experiences and growth. As they prepare to leave this magical place, they express gratitude for the connections they've made and the lessons they've learned. Together, they step through the gate, carrying the memories, love, and bonds forged within the mansion as they venture into the unknown, ready to face whatever life has in store for them.
