In a hidden library within the Moonlit Mansion, four friends, Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper, united by an enchantment, seek to uncover the secrets of their situation. As they delve into ancient texts and scrolls, they discover that the enchantment is tied to love and relationships. The group works tirelessly, piecing together fragments of information, until they find a passage revealing that true love can lift the enchantment. With newfound determination, they face the moonlit night, ready to confront the enchantments head-on and reclaim control of their destinies.
