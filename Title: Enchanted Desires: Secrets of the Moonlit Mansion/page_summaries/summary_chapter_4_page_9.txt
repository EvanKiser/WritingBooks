The four friends find themselves in the Moonlit Mansion's conservatory, a lush oasis that offers a stark contrast to the dusty library they had been studying in. As they wander among the vibrant plants, they share moments of vulnerability and introspection, discussing the enchantment that brought them together and the connections they've formed. Surrounded by the beauty of the conservatory, they embrace their newfound bond and the hidden depths within themselves, feeling stronger together. As they step forward into the unknown, they are guided by the enchanting power of the Moonlit Mansion and the unbreakable love and support of their newfound family, certain that they have discovered more than just ancient secrets and magical enchantments.
