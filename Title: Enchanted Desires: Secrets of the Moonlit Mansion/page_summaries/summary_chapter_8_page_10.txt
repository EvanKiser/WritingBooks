The four companions leave the enchanted Moonlit Mansion and walk through the forest, reflecting on their journey and personal growth. They realize that true strength and beauty lie in the connections they've forged with one another. As they reach an open hillside, they decide to commemorate their experience by returning to the spot every year to celebrate their friendships and remember the lessons they've learned. United by their unforgettable journey, they pledge to uphold this tradition, knowing they will carry the memories and lessons from the Moonlit Mansion with them forever.
