Four strangers enter the grand ballroom of the Moonlit Mansion, where they are captivated by its opulence and enchanting atmosphere. As the night progresses, they find themselves drawn to one another, engaging in deep conversations about their hidden desires and dreams. The mansion's spellbinding aura allows them to explore their emotions and embark on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. The night serves as a prelude to the tales of passion and growth that will unfold within the mysterious Moonlit Mansion.
