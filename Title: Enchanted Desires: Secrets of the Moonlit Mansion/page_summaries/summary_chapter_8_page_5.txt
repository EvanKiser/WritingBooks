The friends venture deeper into the Moonlit Mansion to gather materials for a counter-enchantment, hoping to break the spells that bind them. As they collect the rare herb, blessed water, and locks of each other's hair, they confront their fears and doubts about the consequences of their actions. Despite their worries, they find solace in their unbreakable bond and trust in one another. Together, they prepare to face the counter-enchantment, embracing the unknown and the changes it may bring to their lives.
