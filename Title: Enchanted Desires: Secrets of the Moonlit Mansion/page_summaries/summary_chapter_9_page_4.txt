In the serene setting beneath an ancient oak tree, Elena and Liam share a heartfelt conversation about the personal growth they've experienced in the Moonlit Mansion. Meanwhile, Valarie and Jasper navigate a hedge maze, discussing the fragility of identity and the impact of their shared experiences. As the four friends reunite, they reflect on the deeper connections they've forged and the lessons they've learned. With a renewed sense of determination, they vow to face the future together, confident in the strength of their love and friendship.
