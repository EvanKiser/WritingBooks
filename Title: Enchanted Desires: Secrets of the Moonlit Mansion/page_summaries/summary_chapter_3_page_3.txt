Elena, Valarie, Liam, and Jasper explore the secret library in the Moonlit Mansion, seeking knowledge for a powerful ritual. As they bond and share their fears and desires, they prepare for the enchantment that will change their lives forever. On the night of the ritual, they stand united, ready to face the unknown consequences of the magic they are about to unleash. As they recite the ancient words and raise their crystals, the enchantment weaves a tapestry around them, binding them together for eternity.
