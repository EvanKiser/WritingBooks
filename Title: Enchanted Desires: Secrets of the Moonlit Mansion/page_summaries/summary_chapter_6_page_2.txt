The four companions, Valarie, Hugo, Elena, and Liam, find themselves in a library, seeking answers to an enchantment that affects their emotions and relationships. Valarie and Hugo discuss the authenticity of her feelings for Jasper, while Elena and Liam explore the possibility that their connection is a result of the enchantment. Encouraged by their conversations, they band together to find a solution, their love and friendship guiding them. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they notice a mysterious figure watching them from the shadows, hinting at a connection to the Moonlit Mansion's past. Despite the potential consequences, the companions remain determined to face the challenges together.
