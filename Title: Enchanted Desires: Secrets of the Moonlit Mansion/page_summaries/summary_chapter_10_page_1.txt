Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper, all transformed by their shared experiences at the Moonlit Mansion, have developed a strong bond and newfound confidence. As they walk together towards the bookstore, they reflect on the positive changes in their lives and how their time at the Mansion has given them clarity and purpose. Liam, embracing his new role as a mentor, helps younger students with algebra homework, while the others continue to the bookstore, grateful for their unbreakable friendships and ready to face the next chapter of their lives.
