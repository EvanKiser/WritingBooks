Under the full moon, Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper gather in the Moonlit Mansion's library to perform an enchantment using ancient texts. As they prepare, they bond over their shared feelings of longing for something more in life and the weight of their families' expectations. Once they begin the ritual, a growing warmth and connection between them awaken dormant desires. As the enchantment progresses, Elena and Liam, as well as Valarie and Jasper, feel an irresistible pull towards each other, setting the stage for a magical and life-changing experience.
