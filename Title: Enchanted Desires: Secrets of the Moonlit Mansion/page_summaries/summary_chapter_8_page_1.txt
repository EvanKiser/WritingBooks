In Moonlit Mansion, four friends - Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper - are ensnared by enchantments that manipulate their emotions and desires. Determined to regain control of their lives, they search the mansion's secret library for a counter-enchantment. As they confront their fears and uncertainties, Valarie discovers an ancient tome that holds the key to unraveling the enchantments and untangling their web of emotions.
