After a night of magic and enchantments at the Moonlit Mansion, Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper find themselves in the serene gardens, feeling a deep connection with one another. They share their thoughts on the powerful emotions and desires awakened within them during their adventure in the hidden library. As they rest beneath an old oak tree, they discuss the lasting impact of the magic they experienced and how it has changed their lives. With renewed purpose, they continue their journey together, embracing the magic that now guides them towards their greatest desires and dreams.
