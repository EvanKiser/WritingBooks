Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper find themselves in a hidden library filled with mysterious tomes and enchantments. As they explore the library and discuss the magic they've discovered, they form a strong bond and a sense of camaraderie. Aware of the great power and responsibility that comes with these enchantments, they resolve to treat the magic with respect and not lose sight of their own values. In the magical atmosphere of the Moonlit Mansion, they begin an unforgettable adventure together.
