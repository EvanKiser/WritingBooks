Sunlight illuminates the Moonlit Mansion's library, where four friends - Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper - are determined to uncover the secrets hidden within ancient texts. Each friend delves into their own studies, finding inspiration and purpose in the stories of enchantresses, noble men and women, and hidden paths. As they learn from the past, their destinies become intertwined, and they prepare to embark on the next phase of their journey. Guided by the wisdom of the past and their unbreakable bonds, the friends step out into the world, their fates forever bound to the enchanted Moonlit Mansion.
