Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper enter the Moonlit Mansion's library to uncover its hidden secrets. As they explore the ancient tomes and scrolls, each discovers something about their past, their bloodline, or the history of the mansion that connects them to powerful enchantresses and ancient wisdom. As they share their findings, they realize their destinies are intertwined, and they resolve to continue their quest to unlock their true potential. The library, filled with whispers of the past, seems to acknowledge their determination as a new generation of seekers.
