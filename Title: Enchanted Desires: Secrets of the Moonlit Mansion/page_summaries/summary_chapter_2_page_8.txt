Elena, Liam, Valarie, and Jasper perform an ancient ritual in a hidden library, using candles infused with the essences of earth, air, fire, and water to unlock the hidden power of the tomes. As the enchantments build, they feel a sense of destiny and their fates becoming intertwined. With the ritual completed, they resolve to embrace the enchantments and allow them to shape their lives, forging a new path guided by the power of the Moonlit Mansion.
