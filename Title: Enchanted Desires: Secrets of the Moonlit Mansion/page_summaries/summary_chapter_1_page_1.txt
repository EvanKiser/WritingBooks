In the mysterious Moonlit Mansion, four individuals are drawn together by a mysterious invitation. Elena Morales, a quiet and introverted book-lover, is encouraged by her friend Isabelle to take a risk and attend the event. Liam Grant, a high school football star, seeks to explore his hidden desires away from his athletic persona, with support from his friend Jackson. Valarie Sinclair, an enigmatic beauty from a wealthy family, hopes to escape her monotonous life and find genuine connection. Lastly, Jasper Everett, a privileged millionaire's son, longs for something more than his superficial life and is urged by his valet, Thomas, to indulge in this mysterious world.
