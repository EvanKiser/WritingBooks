The four friends, bound together by a mysterious energy, work together to decipher a counter-enchantment in the eerie yet comforting atmosphere of the Moonlit Mansion's library. As they split into pairs and delve into the ancient tome, they share emotional conversations, exploring their desires, identities, and the love that has blossomed between them. With the midnight hour approaching and the urgency to break the enchantments growing stronger, they find strength in their love and friendship, determined to face the challenges and sacrifices that lie ahead.
