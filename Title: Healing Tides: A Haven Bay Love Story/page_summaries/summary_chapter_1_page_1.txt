Emma Thompson and her best friend Layla Davis move into a charming seaside cottage in Haven Bay, a picturesque coastal city, to start a new chapter in their lives. As Emma focuses on healing her emotional wounds through painting, the two friends explore the town and its art scene, shops, and beaches. Despite the occasional resurfacing of memories from her toxic past relationship, Emma finds solace in the beauty of her new surroundings and the support of her best friend.
